French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 (1 inch thick) slices King's Hawaiian® Sweet Bread
  • 4 large eggs large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons Butter or oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons Maple syrup

Directions

  1. Step 1 Slice bread crosswise so that each slice is about 1-inch thick. Cut larger slices into halves or thirds, if desired. Set aside.
  2. Step 2 In a shallow mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon.
  3. Step 3 Quickly dip slices (do not soak) in egg mixture and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Step 4 Keep egg mixture stirred and spices well blended.
  5. Step 5 Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with warm maple syrup.
